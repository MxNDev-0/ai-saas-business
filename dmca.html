DMCA / Copyright Takedown Policy

Last updated: May 2026

MCN Engine respects intellectual property rights and complies with applicable copyright laws, including the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used on MCN Engine without permission, you may submit a takedown request with the following information:

• Your full name and contact information
• Description of the copyrighted work
• URL or location of the infringing content
• A statement that you believe the use is unauthorized
• A statement that the information you provide is accurate

Content Removal

Upon receiving a valid request, we will review and may remove or restrict access to the reported content.

Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly violate copyright rules may have their accounts suspended or permanently removed.

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification for review.
