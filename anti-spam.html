Anti-Spam Policy

Last updated: May 2026

MCN Engine strictly prohibits spam, abuse, and misleading activities to ensure a safe experience for all users.

Prohibited Activities

• Sending repeated, unsolicited, or bulk messages
• Posting misleading, deceptive, or fake content
• Automated bot activity or system abuse
• Promotion of scams, fraud, or harmful services
• Manipulating engagement or platform features

Monitoring & Detection

We use automated systems and internal monitoring tools to detect suspicious behavior, including unusual activity patterns and abuse signals.

Enforcement

Accounts that violate this policy may be:
• Temporarily restricted
• Permanently suspended
• Removed without prior notice

Reporting Abuse

Users can report spam or abuse through available platform tools or by contacting support.
